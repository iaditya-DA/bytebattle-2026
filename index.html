Got Questions?

Who can participate?

Anyone! Whether you're a seasoned professional, a college student, or a coding beginner, ByteBattle is open to all passionate builders globally.

What if I don't have a team?

Don't worry! We'll have dedicated team-building sessions before the hacking begins so you can find the perfect teammates.

Are there team size limits?

Teams can consist of 1 to 4 members. You can hack solo, but we highly recommend forming a team for the best experience!
